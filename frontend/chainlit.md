# Custom Deep Research on Red Hat OpenShift AI

**AI-powered custom deep research** with multi-agent harness engineering on Red Hat OpenShift AI.

Upload your documents and ask complex research questions. The harness engine iterates until quality meets your threshold.

---

### How It Works

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Plan** | Generates a research strategy with optimized search queries |
| **Execute** | Searches documents, retrieves context, and drafts a report |
| **Verify** | Scores quality, validates citations, checks facts (LLM-as-Judge) |
| **Reflect** | Analyzes failures and improves for the next iteration |

---

### Quick Start

1. **Upload** a PDF or text document using the attachment button below
2. **Ask** a research question about your document
3. **Watch** the harness iterate in real-time via progress steps

---

### Settings

Adjust via the settings panel (gear icon):
- **Quality Threshold** — Higher values trigger more iterations for better output
- **Max Iterations** — Safety limit on how many passes the harness takes
